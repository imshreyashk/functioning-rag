ACME COMPANY POLICIES
 
REFUND POLICY
Customers may return any product within 30 days of purchase for a full refund.
Items must be in original condition. Digital products cannot be refunded.
To initiate a refund, contact support@acme.com with your order number.
Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days.
 
SHIPPING POLICY
Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days within the United States.
Express shipping (2-day) costs $15 and is available for orders over $50.
International shipping takes 10-14 business days.
Free shipping applies to all orders over $75.
 
PRIVACY POLICY
We collect name, email, and purchase history to process orders.
We never sell customer data to third parties.
You can request deletion of your data by emailing privacy@acme.com.
All data is encrypted using AES-256 standard.
